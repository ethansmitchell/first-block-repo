Body Systems B250 Free Weight Bench

Overview

The cornerstone of all strength training equipment is a free weight bench and nothing compares to the B250. The extra heavy-duty 2" x 3" steel frame can easily handle a load capacity of 1,000 lbs. Align your body for perfect posture in all your exercises while the ultra-thick upholstery supports you comfortably through the most intense workouts. Lock your legs in the t-bar for extra comfort and stability.

The B250 assists you in performing upper and lower abdominal exercises as well as incline and decline strength training. Convenient casters let you move the B250 effortlessly in and out of any power rack. The B250 is perfect for for dumbbell exercises, power racks, multi-press racks and smith machines. The B250 can also be used for sit-ups, crunches and leg raises.

Features

  • Quality:

    The B250 has full commercial quality with over 1000 lbs. capacity.

  • Adjustable:

    • The B250 supports a quick, easy, solid and secure 6 position ladder-style back pad adjustment

  • Comfort:

    • The B250 includes a t-bar leg hold down with oversized rollers for extra comfort and stability
