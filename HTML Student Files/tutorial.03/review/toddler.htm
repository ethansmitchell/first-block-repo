For Kids

Arcadium provides attractions for all ages. Opening this summer for families with young children, we present the toddler park, with safe, age specific, rides. At this section of Arcadium, your youngsters can:

  • Ride the Arcadium Camp Bus and tour the sites of the park
  • Watch the Howard Hog review, our popular show with fun characters and songs
  • Play in the world's largest ball pit
  • Enjoy Arcadium minature water park and kiddie pool
  • Play at our 36-hole minature golf course

All toddler areas are supervised by our experienced and friendly staff.
